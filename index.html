About This Site

Built by a student, for students.

Hi — I'm a high school junior with a long-standing passion for mathematics, especially number sense. I've been involved in number sense competitions since elementary school, and over the years it has become one of the most meaningful parts of my academic journey. Math and number sense gave me a foundation I continue to build on through advanced calculus and statistics.

As I progressed, I noticed a consistent challenge: most available resources for number sense were either locked behind paywalls or too generic to offer meaningful guidance. I often wished for a more accessible, structured, and student-friendly way to practice and truly understand the strategies behind number sense.

So I built this. The goal is to provide free, high-quality resources that not only offer practice problems, but help students build intuition, learn techniques, and improve their speed and accuracy — for both students training independently and instructors preparing their teams.

The site is under constant improvement and I'm actively working to expand its content. I can say personally that having something like this would have helped me enormously during my own competition preparation.
